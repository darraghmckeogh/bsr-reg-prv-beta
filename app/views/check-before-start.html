Check before you start

Check you have the information you need

Fire and smoke controls

We will ask you about:

  • the evacuation strategy for the building
  • fire and smoke control provisions and equipment
  • how the fire and smoke controls can be accessed

Energy supply and storage

We need to know what types of energy supply and energy storage are in the building.

Connected buildings

AWAITING CONTENT

Roof

We will ask:

  • whether the roof is flat or pitched
  • whether it has a separate layer of insulation
  • what material the roof is mainly made of

Staircases

We need to know:

  • how many staircases are in the building
  • how many of those serve all the floors above ground level

Exernal walls and features

We’ll ask about the materials used and how much of each material is used on the outside of the building.

We’ll also ask about insulation in the external walls.

We also need to know about external features like balconies, communal recreation areas or walkways, escape routes on the roof, roof lights or masts, solar shading or external stair cores.

What the building is used for

We need to know what the building is used for.

If there are any floors below ground level, we need to know what these are used for.

Changes since construction

We need to know about the most recent changes made to the building since completion.

This could be:

  • changes of use
  • material changes

We will also ask when the most recent changes were made.
