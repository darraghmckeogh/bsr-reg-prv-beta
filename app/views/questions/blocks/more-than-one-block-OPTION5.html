Is {{ data['building-name'] }} a single high-rise residential structure?

A high-rise residential structure has 7 floors or more, or is 18 metres in height or more, and has at least 2 residential units.

High-rise residential structures connected together by a walkway, lobby or basement that contains a residential unit or, a wall containing normal use doors should be registered as a single building with multiple structures.

High-rise residential structures connected together by a walkway, lobby or basement, that does not contain a residential unit or, a wall containing no normal use doors should be registered as different buildings.

For example, The Gherkin in London is a single high-rise residential structure.

Deansgate Square in Manchester is made up of four high-rise residential structures.
