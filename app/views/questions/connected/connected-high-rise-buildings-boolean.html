Connected buildings

Connections to other high-rise residential buildings

These buildings have 7 floors or more, or are 18 metres in height or more, and have at least 2 residential units.

This helps us to determine if a fire could spread between buildings.

A building may be connected to another by a shared wall, basement, lobby or walkway.
