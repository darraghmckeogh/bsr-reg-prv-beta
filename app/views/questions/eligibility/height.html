Building height

Measure the building in metres from ground level to the top floor.

Do not measure:

  • the roof
  • any floor that is only used for machinery or plant rooms
Help with ground level

Ground level is the level of land immediately next to the building. If that land is uneven, it is the lowest part of the land immediately next to the building.
