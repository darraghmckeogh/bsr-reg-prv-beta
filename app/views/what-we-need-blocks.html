Tell us about each high-rise residential structure in {{ data['building-name'] }}

A high-rise residential structure has:

  • at least 7 floors or is at least 18 metres in height
  • at least 2 residential units

We will ask you for information 1 structure at a time. You can tell us about the other structures later.

For each structure, you must tell us:

  • the name of the structure
  • the number of floors at or above ground level
  • the height from ground level to the top floor
  • the number of residential units
  • if people are living in it
  • the year it was originally built and ready for people to live in it
  • its address
  • if it has any other addresses
