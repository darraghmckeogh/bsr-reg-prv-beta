Tell us about each of the independent sections in {{ data['building-name'] }}

An independent section is a collection of units in the building that share a main entrance, or can only be accessed by crossing a party wall, walkway, shared lobby or basement.

These independent sections may have different names. For example, 'Hawthorn Shopping Centre' and 'Primrose Apartments'.

We will ask you for information one section at a time. You can tell us about the other sections later.

For each section, you must tell us:

  • the number of floors at or above ground level
  • the height from ground level to the top floor
  • the number of residential units
  • if people are living in it
  • the year it was originally built and ready for people to live in it
  • its address
  • if it has any other addresses
